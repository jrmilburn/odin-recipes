Pizza

Description

Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home. The heat of a hot grill is a perfect match for a professional pizza oven. Learn the tricks for making and grilling pizza from scratch with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

    • Steps

    1. Make the dough
    2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill plates
    3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill
    4. Flip the crust and brush with oil
    5. Top the pizza
